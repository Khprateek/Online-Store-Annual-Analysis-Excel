# 🛒 Online Store — Annual Sales Analysis 2022

> A complete end-to-end Excel data analysis project examining 2022 annual sales data for an online retail store. The goal was to uncover actionable insights across customer demographics, geographies, and channels to drive smarter decisions for 2023.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
![Excel](https://img.icons8.com/color/48/microsoft-excel-2019.png)  
**Microsoft Excel** — Pivot Tables · Data Cleaning · Dashboard Design · Slicers

---

## 🔄 Workflow

```
Data Collection → Data Cleaning → Data Processing → Pivot Tables → Dashboard & Insights
```

| Stage | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Data Collection** | Sourced raw transactional records from provided CSV dataset |
| **Data Cleaning** | Removed nulls, blanks, duplicates; standardized data types and formats |
| **Data Processing** | Engineered two new features: `Age Group` and `Month` for richer segmentation |
| **Pivot Tables** | Built 6 pivot tables summarizing sales by month, gender, state, age, channel & status |
| **Dashboard & Insights** | Assembled interactive Excel dashboard with 3 slicers (Month, Category, Channel) |

---

## ❓ Business Questions Explored

- 📈 How do monthly sales and order volumes compare across the year?
- 📅 Which month recorded the highest sales and orders?
- 🚻 Do men or women purchase more?
- 📊 What is the breakdown of order statuses?
- 🏢 Which top 10 states contribute most to sales?
- 👥 How do age and gender influence order volume?
- 🌐 Which sales channels drive the most revenue?
- 🏆 What is the highest-selling product category?

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Peak Months:** February and March recorded the highest orders and revenue
- **Gender Split:** Women accounted for ~64% of total purchases
- **Top States:** Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh contributed ~35% of sales
- **Age Group:** Adults aged 31–50 drove ~50% of all orders
- **Dominant Channels:** Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra together = ~80% of revenue
- **Fulfillment Rate:** 92% of all orders were successfully delivered

---

## 🎯 Recommendations

- **High-Value Segment:** Target women aged 30–49 in Maharashtra, Karnataka & Uttar Pradesh with personalized promotions
- **Channel Strategy:** Increase ad spend on Amazon, Flipkart & Myntra during Jan–March to capitalize on peak demand
- **Retention:** Introduce loyalty programs for the adult age group (31–50) to improve repeat purchases
